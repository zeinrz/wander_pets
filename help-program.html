Pet Qualifications

  • The pet is at least 3 months old
  • The pet must be healthy and show no signs of illness or disease at the time of vaccination.
  • The pet have not been bite or scratch anyone for the past months
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding pets are typically advised against vaccination.
  • The pet have not been taken any anti-rabies vaccination for more than a year
  • The owner or a responsible handler must be present and capable of safely and properly restraining the pet during the vaccination process, using a leash or carrier.
