About Wander Pets!

We are a volunteer-based animal welfare NGO.

Our primary mission is to provide shelter for all animals, leaving no harmed animals behind. We also educate and spread information about animal welfare, care, and control, as well as other animal-related issues.

Wander Pets Organisation actively fights animal cruelty and pet neglect, including dogfighting, horsefighting, and the use of wild animals for entertainment.

Mission

The Wander Pets helps to prevent cruelty, alleviate pain, fear and suffering in animals and to promote a society based on humane principles. We actively connect with communities to promote kindness, knowledge, and moral in addition to saving animals in fear and suffering.

Vision

Wander Pets is a credible non-profit organization composed of a community of committed, caring, and and honest individuals and institutions that initiate and lead in the promotion of animal welfare and the protection of all animals.

Values

  • Compassion and respect for animals.
  • Commitment to our vision and mission
  • Dedication to the assigned role in the organization.
  • Respectful conduct towards each other regardless of position.
  • Dicipline and trustworthiness to the organization.
